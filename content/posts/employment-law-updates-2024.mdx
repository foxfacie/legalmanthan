# Major Employment Law Updates in India 2024

The Indian employment landscape has witnessed significant regulatory changes in 2024, affecting both employers and employees across various sectors.

## Key Updates

### New Labor Codes
Implementation of consolidated labor codes affecting wages, social security, and industrial relations.

### Remote Work Policies
Updated guidelines for work-from-home arrangements and digital workplace regulations.

### Gender Equality Measures
Enhanced provisions for workplace harassment prevention and gender pay equity.

### Gig Economy Regulations
New frameworks governing platform workers and freelance employment.
