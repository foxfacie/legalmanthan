# Corporate Governance Best Practices for Indian Companies

Effective corporate governance is crucial for building investor confidence and ensuring long-term business sustainability in India's evolving regulatory landscape.

## Key Governance Principles

### Board Independence
Maintaining an optimal balance of independent and executive directors.

### Transparency
Regular disclosure of material information to stakeholders.

### Accountability
Clear responsibility structures and performance metrics.

### Risk Management
Comprehensive risk assessment and mitigation strategies.
